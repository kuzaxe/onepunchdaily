Help & common questions

The hero routine, scaled to you. If something's broken or you have an idea, email raj.pandya@outlook.com.
How do I change my starting level?

Settings → Program → Starting Level

I missed a day. Did I lose my progress?

No. Miss one day and you repeat the current level next week — no setback. The program is designed to be forgiving.

Can I skip the run?

Yes. Settings → Exercises — toggle off any movement you don't want.

How do I change my rest day?

Settings → Rest Days — pick any day of the week.

Where's the widget?

Long-press your Home Screen → Add Widget → search "One Punch Daily."

How do reminders work?

Settings → Notifications — pick a time for the daily nudge and optionally enable a "Streak Save" alert later in the evening.

Still stuck?

Email raj.pandya@outlook.com and I'll get back to you.
